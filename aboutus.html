ABOUT US

THE SPIRIT OF MOONFEST

Moonfest isn’t just a music festival — it’s a ritual of sound, light, and connection. Under the open sky and glowing moon, we gather to celebrate a shared love for rhythm, movement, and unforgettable moments.

Founded on the belief that music transcends language, Moonfest brings together a global lineup of visionary artists — from legendary icons to rising stars. Each stage is a canvas, each set a story in sound.

But Moonfest is more than the music. It’s a community. A campsite of laughter, light shows, and late-night conversations. A celebration of art, expression, and freedom — where strangers become friends and the ordinary becomes magical.

We evolve each year, but our core remains the same: to create a transformative experience that lives on in memory long after the last beat drops.
