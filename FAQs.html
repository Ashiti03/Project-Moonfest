FAQs

1. Tickets & Admission

Q: How do I purchase tickets for the festival?
A: Tickets can be purchased exclusively through our official website, on the Tickets page. We strongly advise against purchasing from unauthorized third-party sellers.
Q: What types of tickets are available?
A: We offer various ticket types including General Admission (GA) 1-Day, GA Weekend, VIP 1-Day, and VIP Weekend passes. Please see our tickets page for full details and pricing.
Q: Are tickets refundable or transferable?
A: All ticket sales are final. We do not offer refunds or exchanges unless the event is cancelled or rescheduled. Transfers are not possible.

2. Entry & Re-Entry

Q: What time do gates open each day?
A: For gate opening times, we will send you an official email, 1 or 2 days prior to the show. In case you do not recieve the email, please inform us at contact.support@moonfest.com.
Q: What should I bring for entry?
A: Please bring your valid ticket (printed or on your mobile device) and a valid government-issued photo ID.
Q: Is re-entry allowed?
A: No, re-entry is not permitted once you have exited the festival grounds each day. Please check the specific policy on your ticket type.

3. Getting There & Parking

Q: How can I get to the festival?
A: We recommend using public transport, ride-sharing services, or our official shuttle service. Detailed directions and public transport routes can be found on the venue.
Q: Is parking available?
A: Limited parking will be available on a first-come, first-served basis for a fee. We strongly encourage carpooling or using alternative transportation due to limited space.
Q: Is there a designated pick-up/drop-off area for ride-shares or taxis?
A: Yes, there will be a clearly marked pick-up/drop-off zone near the main entrance. Please follow signage upon arrival.
